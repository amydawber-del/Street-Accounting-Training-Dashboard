What is the Confidence Score?
A self-reported score (1–10) submitted by the learner at the end of the simulator. It reflects how confident they feel applying Street accounting workflows in real client situations.
1–3 · Low — needs follow-up or refresher
4–6 · Building — progressing but uncertain
7–10 · Strong — ready to apply skills
A high progress % with a low confidence score flags a "confidence gap" — the learner has completed the modules but still feels uncertain.
